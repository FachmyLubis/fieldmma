ACADEMY
PLEDGE.

For each student who walks through our doors, we will support and inspire you to achieve your personal best, working towards a Black Belt on the mat and in life. As we train together, we also bring out the best in each other, building confidence and community.

LIFE.
BALANCE.

Martial arts will develop your overall well-being, from fitness to focus. No matter how young the student, training will lead to personal growth built upon hard work, responsibility and self-awareness.
Please take this time to consider the positive influences and role models your family will build upon in life. Training at our academy will provide a lifetime of education and a wealth of friendship for you and your family. 

HIGH EXPECTATIONS.

Our developmental programs encourage attitudes that impact every area of life.

ADULT'S
PROGRAM.

Embrace traditional mixed martial arts! Kickboxing, Karate and Brazilian Jiu-jitsu classes. Train in all 3! Each style is taught separately with its own class time and aligned with a traditional belt ranking system. Training in our adult program provides a complete and balanced understanding of martial arts as a whole and develops a confident, educated martial artist capable in all areas of self-defense.
All 3 programs are offered to our adult members under 1 membership. In addition, we offer morning and evening classes 6 days per week. We provide martial art training as a lifestyle, an education to be shared together with a commitment to building community. This is passed on to our students who become instructors themselves. It is the mindset of service, team and hard work that transcends our traditional mixed martial arts program into a life skill of success, self-improvement and community development.

KIDS PROGRAM.

Our kids program is a unique, structured curriculum designed for children ages 3.5 to 12 years old. It is a developmental curriculum built upon Taekwondo and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu aligned with a traditional belt system. The program is designed to improve physical capabilities and create personal confidence.
The combination of standup striking (karate) and ground defense (Jiu-jitsu) represents a balanced approach to self-defense (Inyodo). Our kids program is characterized by repetitive commands, systemized drilling and a class structure that rewards listening, focus and hard work. Encouraging fun while working hard is a principle in our system, in every class, at every level, at every age!
